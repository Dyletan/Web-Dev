Beetroot, hummus & crispy chickpea sub sandwich

    Ingredient Name Amount
    Cooked Beetroot 300g pack, half sliced
    Chickpeas 400g can, drained
    Vegan Pesto 3 tbsp
    Large Ciabatta Rolls 2, sliced in half
    Mixed Rocket, Watercress & Spinach Salad 2 large handfuls
  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: 1

Step 1: Blitz the whole beetroot, ¾ of the chickpeas, 2 tbsp pesto, and 1 tbsp oil in a food processor with some seasoning until you have a thick, smooth hummus. Heat the ciabatta following the pack instructions.

Step 2:Fry the remaining chickpeas in a little oil until crisp, then set aside. Toss the salad leaves with the remaining pesto and a splash of vinegar. Slice the rolls, then assemble the sandwiches with the hummus, beetroot slices, salad leaves, and fried chickpeas.

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

    Ingredient Name Amount
    Spaghetti 200g
    Garlic 3 cloves, minced
    Extra Virgin Olive Oil 1/3 cup
    Red Pepper Flakes 1/2 teaspoon
    Fresh Parsley Chopped, for garnish
  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 2

Step 1: Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Reserve a cup of pasta water before draining.

Step 2: In a pan, sauté minced garlic in olive oil until golden. Add red pepper flakes for some heat.

Step 3: Toss the cooked spaghetti in the garlic-infused oil. If too dry, add reserved pasta water gradually.

Step 4: Garnish with chopped fresh parsley before serving.

Vegetarian Tacos

    Ingredient Name Amount
    Black Beans 1 can, drained and rinsed
    Corn Tortillas 8
    Avocado Sliced
    Cherry Tomatoes Halved
    Shredded Lettuce 1 cup
  • Time: 25 min
  • Serves: 4

Step 1: Warm the black beans in a pan over medium heat.

Step 2: Heat the corn tortillas on a dry skillet until pliable.

Step 3: Assemble the tacos with black beans, sliced avocado, halved cherry tomatoes, and shredded lettuce.

Step 4: Serve with your favorite salsa or hot sauce.
